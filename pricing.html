What is the AI Website Builder? +
Our AI Website Builder creates a complete website for your business in minutes. You can preview it for free before deciding to purchase.
How much does a website cost? +
Our plans start at ₹4,999 for the Starter plan, ₹9,999 for Business, and ₹19,999 for Professional. The AI Business Package is ₹29,999.
Do you offer monthly payments? +
Yes, we offer monthly payment options for all our plans. You can also choose one-time payment for the full package.
What is included in the AI Business Package? +
The AI Business Package includes everything: AI Website Generator, AI Content, AI SEO, Firebase Integration, Groq API Ready, Admin Dashboard, Lead CRM, and more.
Is there a free trial? +
Yes, you can use the AI Website Builder to generate a free demo website. You can preview it before making any payment.

No, our pricing is completely transparent. The price you see is the price you pay. No hidden fees.

Most websites are delivered within 24-48 hours. Complex projects may take 3-5 days.
Can I upgrade my plan later? +
Yes, you can upgrade your plan anytime. We'll adjust the pricing accordingly.
Do you provide support? +
Yes, all plans come with dedicated support. Business and Professional plans include 3-6 months of support.
What is your refund policy? +
We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund your payment.

Introduction

Last Updated: January 2025

Welcome to Smile AI Web Studio. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with us.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using our website and services, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as described in this policy.

Our Commitment: We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly and securely.

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, business name, and other contact details you provide.
  • Website Information: Information about your business, industry, website requirements, and preferences.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our website, pages visited, time spent, and referring URLs.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device type.
  • Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies to enhance your experience and analyze website traffic.

How We Use Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To generate AI-powered website demos and content
  • To communicate with you about your inquiries and projects
  • To improve our website, services, and user experience
  • To send updates, marketing communications, and promotional offers
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

Cookies Policy

We use cookies to enhance your experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and improve your user experience.

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain features on our website.

We use both session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which remain on your device for a longer period).

Third Party Services

We use third-party services to enhance our platform and provide better user experiences. These include:

  • Vercel: For website hosting and deployment
  • Firebase/Firestore: For database and authentication services
  • Google Analytics: For website traffic analysis and performance tracking
  • Google Search Console: For SEO and search performance monitoring
  • Payment Gateways: For secure transaction processing (Razorpay, PhonePe, etc.)

These third-party services have their own privacy policies and data handling practices. We encourage you to review them.

Firebase Usage

We use Google Firebase and Firestore to manage user data, store information, and provide real-time functionality. Firebase helps us:

  • Store user preferences and account information
  • Manage website requests and project data
  • Enable secure authentication and authorization
  • Provide real-time updates and notifications

All data stored in Firebase is encrypted and protected according to industry standards.

AI Services

Our AI-powered website generator uses artificial intelligence to create content, design layouts, and generate SEO meta tags. When you use our AI services:

  • Your business information is used to generate personalized website content
  • AI analysis is performed to optimize your website for search engines
  • Data may be processed through AI APIs (including Groq, OpenAI, Gemini, and Claude)
  • All data processing is performed securely and in compliance with privacy regulations

Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure storage and encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Employee training on data protection and privacy
Security Assurance: We continuously update our security practices to protect your information and comply with industry standards.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Restriction: Restrict the processing of your data
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another provider
  • Object: Object to the processing of your data for marketing purposes

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us, and we will take appropriate action to remove the information.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or industry standards. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

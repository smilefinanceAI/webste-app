Frequently Asked

What is website development? +
Website development is the process of creating functional, responsive, and user-friendly websites for businesses and organizations. It includes planning, design, coding, testing, and deployment.
How much does website development cost? +
Our website development plans start at ₹999 for basic business websites, ₹2999 for business websites, and ₹4999 for premium websites with advanced features.
Do you provide a free demo? +
Yes, we provide a free website demo before any payment. Fill the form or contact us to get started.
How long does it take to develop a website? +
Most websites are delivered within 24-48 hours. Custom applications and complex projects take 5-10 days depending on requirements.
Is the website mobile responsive? +
Absolutely. All our websites are fully responsive and work seamlessly on all devices including desktops, tablets, and smartphones.
Do you provide SEO with the website? +
Yes, we build SEO-ready websites with proper meta tags, schema markup, and structure to help you rank well on search engines.
Can I update the website myself? +
Yes, we provide easy-to-use content management systems (CMS) or can train you to manage and update your website content.
Do you offer maintenance services? +
Yes, we offer website maintenance plans including regular updates, backups, security monitoring, and performance optimization.
What industries do you work with? +
We work with restaurants, clinics, schools, finance, real estate, retail, manufacturing, travel, NGOs, and many more industries.
